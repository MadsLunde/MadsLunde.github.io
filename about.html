What is the purpose of this website?

To share and receive annonymous thoughts from people around the world

Why this website?

The basic concept was inspired by the "/r/ShowerThoughts" subreddit.
I created this webiste in hopes of learning the basics of HTML, CSS, Javascript, creating an ASP.NET Core backend and SQL Database.
As this is my very first website, I decided to only use vanilla CSS and Javascript, to fully understand the fundementals before voyaging into frameworks.

If you find any bugs, or manage to break something, please do not hesistate to contact me. You will find various ways to contact me on the "Contact"-page.
Any feedback will be appreciated.
